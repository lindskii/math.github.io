The 11th International Conference on Numerical Optimization and Numerical Linear Algebra

The 11th International Conference on Numerical Optimization and Numerical Linear Algebra
August 8-11, 2017, Yinchuan, Ningxia, China

 

Conference Introduction

The International Conference on Numerical Optimization and Numerical Linear Algebra will be held in the city of Yinchuan, Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region, China, on August 8-11, 2017. This is the 11th of the biennial conference series, with previous ones held in Qingdao (1997), Nanjing (1999), Dunhuang (2001), Guilin (2003), Lhasa (2005), Urumuqi (2007), Lijiang (2009), Xiamen (2011), Changchun (2013) and Yanan (2015). The Conference will be organized by the State Key Laboratory of Scientific and Engineering Computing, Institute of Computational Mathematics and Scientific/Engineering Computing, Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science, Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Beifang University of Nationalities. The sponsors of the conference include the National Natural Science Foundation of China and the Chinese Mathematical Society, besides the organizers. Invited lectures will be given on recent advances of numerical optimization, numerical linear algebra, and their applications.
